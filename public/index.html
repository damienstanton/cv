Why I love computing

My goal is bringing AI-driven apps to the field of IT Ops. To this end, I have spent nearly the entirety of my professional career working to help automate systems and make it easier to understand and visualize data. Having used many languages, many tools and many systems over the years I have settled on a workflow using TypeScript, Go, C# and Python ecosystems. I believe that AI an new computer interfaces such as Oculus Rift and Hololens can fundamentally change how people manage complex hardware and software systems. My own consulting company is heavily investing in this technology and cannot wait to explore what we can all do with it.
